We Are TikiSoul

Launched in February 2019 by four friends out of South Central Los Angeles with a plan to infuse food with a different fun vibe, TikiSoul is known for its Pineapple Bowl, Teriyaki Tacos, one-of-a-kind Pineapple Fried Rice, and Pineapple Lemonade. It's arguably the best in LA, people can't get enough! TikiSoul has participated in the Orange County Black History Fair, catered family reunions, special events, and more. We continue to have pop-ups and infuse soul into every palate throughout Southern California.
